Chadwick Aaron Boseman

Chadwick Boseman as Black Panther, the greatest king of Wakanda.

The King's Life

  • 1976- born November, 29th in Anderson, South Carolina, USA
  • 1995- graduated from T. L. Hanna High School. He played in his school's Basketball team. He wrote his first play, 'Crossroads', after his friend was shot and killed.
  • 2000- graduated from Howard University in Washington, D.C. with a Bachelor of Fine Arts.
  • 2000-2007-He started his early career, living in New York.
  • 2008-2015-in 2008 he moved to Los Angeles to further pursue his film and acting career.
  • 2016-2019- he entered into the Marvel Comic Universe and acted in his iconic movie Black Panther as T'Challa and other great movies such as Marshall and 21 Bridges.
  • 2020- He was diagnosed with stage III colon Cancer in 2016. Unfortunately it progessed to stage IV by 2020. He died in Los Angeles aged 43.
